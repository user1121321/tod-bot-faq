Discord Truth or Dare Bot Site

This bot is a basic truth or dare bot. Nothing special.

Here are the commands:

  • $truth - Sends a truth prompt.
  • $dare - Sends a dare prompt.
  • $tod - Sends either a truth or a dare prompt.
  • $help - Lists all commands.

Form to add more questions to Tod.
